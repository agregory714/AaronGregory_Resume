Chemistry⚗️

My name is Aaron Gregory and I am currently a senior at UC Riverside. Before being at UCR, I was at Mt. San Jacinto Community College where I was a student for four years. There, I workded as a Supplemental Instructor where I assisted students in General Chemistry for Dr. John Schuler. As an SI, I facilitated group learning and assisted those that came to session in which I held twice a week. After a semester of being an SI, I moved to become a peer tutor. There, I was allowed to help a multitude of students with any topic concerning the Math & Sciences. Being a tutor for one year, I obtained a Tutor level 1 certificate, which is awarded to those with multiple hours of tutoring fellow students. After being a tutor for one year, I transferred to UCR. Since my transfer, I joined Dr. Vincent Lavallo's research lab where I was fortunate enough to conduct actual scientific research and improve my knowledge of Chemistry. As a result of my time in the lab, I was made co-author of a Inorganic Chemistry paper published in the Journal of the American Chemistry Society.
